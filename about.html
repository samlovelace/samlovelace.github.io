Sam Lovelace

I received my Bachelors of Science in Aerospace Engineering from Florida Institute of Technology in 2022. I am currently a graduate student at Florida Tech studying Aerospace Engineering with a focus in Control Systems. While my primary interests are in the field of robotics and controls, I have involved myself in projects which allow me to work on different aspects of aerospace engineering such as rocket propulsion and heat transfer. With some colleagues, I continue to perform research into developing a solid propellant propulsion system for small satellites.
